# Product 1 Assembly SOP

**Product Code:** PRODUCT-1  
**Source:** GBrain / Manufacturing / Assembly Procedures  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-01

## Overview

Product 1 is a two-block LEGO stack. Employees must place the red block on top of the black block.

## Safety

- Work on a flat, stable surface
- Keep blocks visible to the verification camera

## Assembly Steps

### Step 1: Place red block on black block

Take the **red block** and place it on top of the **black block**.

Success criteria:
- Red block is centered above the black block
- Stack is stable and visible to the camera
