# Product 2 Assembly SOP

**Product Code:** PRODUCT-2  
**Source:** GBrain / Manufacturing / Assembly Procedures  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-01

## Overview

Product 2 is a two-block LEGO stack. Employees must place the red block on top of the white block.

## Safety

- Work on a flat, stable surface
- Keep blocks visible to the verification camera

## Assembly Steps

### Step 1: Place red block on white block

Take the **red block** and place it on top of the **white block**.

Success criteria:
- Red block is centered above the white block
- Stack is stable and visible to the camera

### Step 2: Place yellow block on red block

Take the **yellow block** and place it on top of the **red block**.

Success criteria:
- Yellow block is centered above the red block
- Full three-block stack is stable
